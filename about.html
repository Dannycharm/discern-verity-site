Praise Daniels

Founder, Discern Verity

Praise is a Chicago-based engineer specializing in vehicle safety data. He holds a B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign and is pursuing a Master of Engineering in Autonomy and Robotics at UIUC.

His research background is in exactly the technology that powers modern fleet safety systems: he co-authored DRAIV, a real-time driver-assistance system that turns camera perception data into safety alerts, published in the Teuscher Lab 2025 Summer Proceedings (Portland State University), and has built and evaluated machine-learning systems as a research fellow with the Chicago Quantum Exchange and as a technical AI training specialist evaluating production AI systems.

Discern Verity applies that background to one problem: small and mid-sized trucking fleets collect enormous amounts of safety data — and get nothing for it at renewal. The cameras detect. The platforms report. Discern Verity is the independent layer that turns that data into coaching a fleet can act on and documentation an underwriter can credit.

Paid by the fleet only: no software commissions, no carrier appointments, no share of anyone's premium.
